Description:
  Create/provision and remove/deprovision a BCM tier to your active BCM cluster.

Available Commands:
  create    Deploy a BCM system container tier from your data center.
  remove    Remove a BCM system container tier from your data center.
  list      List BCM tiers that are currently provisioned to your data center.

Usage:
  bcm deploy COMMAND TIER_NAME

Note: TIER_NAME can be 'bitcoin', 'ui', 'kafka', or 'gateway'.
